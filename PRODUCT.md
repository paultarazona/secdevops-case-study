# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and developers learning web application security in a local, controlled environment. Their job is to understand a vulnerability, observe its impact in V1, and verify the equivalent protection in V2 without needing prior knowledge of the repository structure.

## Product Purpose

SecDevOps Lab turns the paired applications into a guided practice environment. Success means a learner can navigate a challenge, understand its objective and safety boundary, verify the vulnerable and hardened outcomes, and continue to the next lesson.

## Brand Personality

Professional, guided, and evidence-based. The interface should feel like a well-maintained technical lab notebook: calm enough for study, explicit about risk, and never theatrical about security.

## Anti-references

Do not turn the lab into a game dashboard with points, badges, or artificial urgency. Do not use hacker-film decoration, neon terminal aesthetics, dark gradients, glassmorphism, or dense unexplained controls. Do not expose V1 as suitable for public deployment.

## Design Principles

- Teach through verified behavior: opening a lesson is not completing it.
- Keep the vulnerable and protected states comparable without hiding their different safety boundaries.
- Lead learners from context to action to evidence to remediation.
- Preserve the functional application beneath the lesson instead of simulating security concepts.
- Make local-only safety limits visible wherever a learner can run a demonstration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Every lesson must be keyboard usable, expose status changes through live regions, use non-color state cues, support 400% zoom and reduced motion, and use direct language for learners with varied security experience.
